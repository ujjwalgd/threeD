Lucid Dreaming :)


"A lucid dream is a type of dream in which the dreamer becomes aware that they are dreaming"



When you dream , you see a lot of weird , exciting , scary and amazing things . Some don't even make sense but still.. You believe in it .
Ever wonder WHY ? Why do you believe them when you know it's not possible ? Because when we sleep , the part of mind which is responsible for “making sense” is also sleeping .

But imagine if You are able to awaken that part of your brain ! Then you are in another world like a parallel universe , But this time you know that this world is your mind's creation .
You are the GOD of this world . This is lucid dreaming .
